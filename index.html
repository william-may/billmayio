About Me

Professional:

An accomplished, confident Software Engineer with 15 years of development experience specializing in developing web applications primarily been using JAVA with the Spring and Hibernate frameworks.

I have extensive experience in the full lifecycle of the software development lifecycle including requirements definition, prototyping, design, implementation, testing and maintenance.

While I can focus on either the front end or back end of an application I do not shy away from being a full stack developer. I actually quite enjoy the challenges and satisfaction that being a full stack developer brings.

Summary of Qualifications:

  • Expertise in Object-Oriented Analysis/Design and development.
  • Experience with various software development methodologies including Agile, TDD and XP.
  • Strong working knowledge of J2EE with experience in various frameworks.
  • Solid working knowledge of .NET
  • Solid communication skills.

Technical Expertise

  • Languages: Java, C#, ASP.NET, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Frameworks: Hibernate, JSP, Spring, Spring MVC, Struts, Adobe Flex and ASP.NET MVC 3, JQuery
  • Software: Eclipse, Spring Tool Suite, Visual Studio 2008 & 2010, Maven, IBM Rational Team Concert

Personal:

I am a father of four daughters, musician and gamer. My current favorite shows are Game of Thones and The Walking Dead.

Having four daughters has taught me to be an empathetic listener, which is a trait that I value very much. Finally, I try to find humor in every situation I currently am as I have found that a good laugh can get you past most obtacles your currently facing.
